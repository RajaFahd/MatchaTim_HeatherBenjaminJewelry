## **Business Case | AI-Powered Order-to-Production Assistant** 

## **Organisation** 

Heather Benjamin Jewelry creates ethically sourced, handmade, limited-edition, and one-of-a-kind jewelry sold through wholesale accounts, retail events, and online channels. 

The company works with independent artisans and production partners in Bali. As the business grows, many operational processes still depend on the founder’s product knowledge, production expertise, and manual coordination. 

## **Business Challenge** 

Wholesale purchase orders arrive in different formats (PDFs, spreadsheets, emails, and order forms) and often contain multiple unique products, styles, materials, and customer requirements. 

The current process requires significant manual work: 

- Reviewing purchase orders 

- Confirming product codes, descriptions, sizes, materials, and pricing 

- Creating invoices 

- Preparing production instructions 

- Identifying materials needed 

- Tracking deposits and timelines 

- Managing backorders 

- Coordinating with Bali production partners 

- Updating customers 

Critical information is often scattered across spreadsheets, product documents, images, emails, and messages. This creates risks including: 

- Production misunderstandings 

- Incorrect products or quantities 

- Packing mistakes 

- Delayed customer communication 

- Difficulty tracking order progress 

- Reliance on founder knowledge 

## **Core Need** 

The business needs an AI assistant that maintains each purchase order from start to finish: 

**Customer Order → Production → Quality Control → Packing → Shipping → Customer Updates** 

The system should become a central source of truth that converts customer orders into clear instructions for both production partners and the Bali fulfillment team. 

For Bali, the system should provide an organized packing and fulfillment guide containing: 

- Customer and purchase order details 

- Product images 

- Style codes 

- Product descriptions 

- Quantities 

- Sizes and variations 

- Materials/specifications 

- Special requests 

- Packaging requirements 

- Missing items or backorders 

- Final packing checklist 

This allows the Bali team to accurately produce, verify, and pack orders without needing additional clarification from the founder. 

## **Goal** 

Create an AI-powered Order-to-Production Assistant that transforms customer purchase orders into a clear, accurate, and trackable operational workflow. 

The solution should reduce administrative workload, improve communication, capture business knowledge, and create a scalable process as the company grows. 

## **Business Objective** 

Reduce order processing and coordination time by 80%. 

Success would include: 

- Faster order processing 

- Fewer production and packing errors 

- Better customer communication 

- Clearer handoffs between teams 

- Improved order visibility 

- Reduced dependence on individual knowledge 

## **Proposed AI Workflow** 

## **1. Order Intake** 

User uploads a customer purchase order. 

AI extracts: 

- Customer details 

- Purchase order number 

- Products and quantities 

- Sizes 

- Materials 

- Pricing 

- Delivery requirements 

## ● Special instructions 

The system creates a digital order record. 

## **2. Order Review** 

AI identifies: 

- Missing information 

- Unclear product references 

- Production concerns 

- Items needing clarification 

## **3. Production Preparation** 

AI generates production instructions with: 

- Product images 

- Style codes 

- Descriptions 

- Quantities 

- Materials 

- Artisan notes 

- Production requirements 

## **4. Order Tracking** 

AI creates a workflow checklist covering: 

- Invoice 

- Deposit tracking 

- Material preparation 

- Production progress 

- Quality control 

- Packing 

- Shipping 

- Customer updates 

## **5. Bali Fulfillment Support** 

AI generates a final packing document showing exactly what should be shipped, including images, quantities, variations, packaging notes, and outstanding items. 

## **Available Data** 

Students can use: 

- Historical purchase orders 

- Product catalogs 

- Product images 

- Pricing information 

- Wholesale line sheets 

- Product descriptions 

- Production procedures 

- Fulfillment checklists 

- Customer communication examples 

- Packing instructions 

- Previous production notes 

## **Constraints** 

- Sensitive customer and financial information will be excluded where possible. 

- Full Shopify, QuickBooks, or ERP integration is not required. 

- Prototype should demonstrate the AI workflow, not replace all business systems. 

- Inventory data may be incomplete. 

## **Expected Outcome** 

Teams should deliver: 

- Working prototype 

- Short demo video 

- Documentation explaining the AI workflow 

- Demonstration of converting a purchase order into a production and fulfillment process 

## **Central Question** 

How can AI transform a customer purchase order into a clear, accurate, and trackable workflow that supports artisans, enables the Bali team to pack correctly, reduces manual coordination, preserves business knowledge, and helps a handmade jewelry business scale?
